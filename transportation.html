Cruise to Island Process

Visitors to Taniti typically arrive aboard a small cruise ship that docks in the calm waters of Yellow Leaf Bay once per week. The arrival process is smooth and family-friendly, with clearly marked disembarkation points and staff available to assist travelers with children or luggage.

Learn More

Shuttles and taxis are readily available at the harbor to transport guests directly to hotels and resorts, keeping costs reasonable and transfers stress-free. Most cruise itineraries connect through major ports originating from Phoenix, and future airport expansion is planned to allow direct commercial flights.

Methods of Transportation

Shuttles & Buses

Public buses operate throughout Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m., offering an affordable and reliable way for families to get around. Private shuttle services serve destinations outside the city, making it easy to reach beaches, resorts, and attractions without renting a car.

Learn More

Taxis & Rentals

Taxis are widely available in Taniti City and are a convenient option for short trips or evenings out. Rental cars can be reserved near the airport for travelers planning volcano or rainforest excursions, though most families find shuttles and taxis more cost-effective.

Learn More

Walking & Bikes

Many areas, including Merriton Landing, are flat and highly walkable, making them ideal for families with strollers. Bike rentals are also available and include helmets; families are encouraged to ask providers about child accommodations before renting.
