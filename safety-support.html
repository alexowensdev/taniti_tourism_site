Emergency Information

Taniti is well-prepared for emergencies, with one main hospital and several smaller clinics staffed by multilingual personnel who can assist families and international travelers. While violent crime is extremely rare, it’s recommended to report any instances of petty theft promptly. Electrical outlets are 120 volts, the same as in the U.S., so travelers won’t need adapters for most electronics.

Hours & Directions

English is widely spoken in tourist areas, and signage is clear in public spaces. Visitors should plan around national holidays, as some medical offices and services may be closed or operate on reduced hours.

Medical Offices

Main Hospital

The 24hr main hospital is fully equipped for family care, emergency response, and routine health needs. Multilingual staff assist travelers, and pediatric and adult care are available.

Directions

Lime Key Office

Located near Taniti City, Lime Key Office offers rapid access for minor illnesses or injuries. Staff are trained to help families with children and provide clear guidance on local pharmacies and follow-up care.

Hours & Directions

Shell Beach Clinic

Shell Beach Clinic serves the Merriton Landing area and offers convenient access to minor medical care and family services. Friendly staff guide visitors on treatment options and provide multilingual support.

Safety Tips

Petty Crime Prevention

Petty theft is rare but can occur in crowded areas. Keep wallets and phones secure, and use hotel safes when available. Always be vigilant when exploring tourist spots.

More

Family Safety

Stick to regulated activities, supervised attractions, and certified guides. Helmets and life jackets are available at biking and water-based activities. Check for age restrictions for some excursions.

More

Health Precautions

Access to multilingual medical staff ensures families can receive care quickly. Drink responsibly (age 18+), wash hands regularly, and follow local health advisories to stay safe during your stay.
