CRUISE INFORMATION

Taniti welcomes cruise ship visitors docking in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. From the harbor, it's easy to transfer to your family-friendly hotel via shuttle or taxi. Enjoy the safe harbor and start your 5-day family vacation exploring sandy beaches, rainforests, and the volcano. Ground transportation includes public buses, taxis, and rental cars available near the airport, but many areas like Merriton Landing are walkable—perfect for families with strollers.

TOP DESTINATIONS

TANITI CITY

Taniti City blends traditional island charm with modern convenience, making it an ideal home base for families. Visitors can explore native architecture inspired by generations of island culture, stroll along white sandy beaches bordering Yellow Leaf Bay, and enjoy calm, shallow waters perfect for young swimmers. While Taniti City is not focused on bustling nightlife or large-scale urban attractions, it offers easy access to family-friendly restaurants, local markets, and budget-conscious lodging options. Its relaxed pace allows families to unwind while still being centrally located for excursions across the island.

MERRITON LANDING

Merriton Landing is Taniti’s most family-focused destination, designed with visitors of all ages in mind. Located on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay, this lively area offers attractions such as guided snorkeling tours, zip-lining through coastal forests, family arcades, and small amusement venues. Wide sidewalks and pedestrian-only zones make it safe and stroller-friendly, allowing families to explore comfortably on foot. In the evenings, parents can relax at nearby pubs or the local microbrewery while enjoying the welcoming atmosphere, with alcohol service responsibly ending at midnight to maintain a family-oriented environment.

BEACHES & VOLCANO

Taniti’s beaches are known for their soft sand, gentle waves, and clear waters, making them ideal for children to safely swim, snorkel, and build sandcastles. Many beaches are monitored and easily accessible, ensuring peace of mind for parents. For a memorable family adventure, visitors can take a guided day trip to Taniti’s small active volcano. Well-marked trails and observation areas allow guests to experience stunning views, unique geology, and educational opportunities without risk. The close proximity of beaches, rainforest paths, and volcanic terrain means families can enjoy diverse natural attractions without long travel times.
